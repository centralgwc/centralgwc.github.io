The Lady's Friends

Chat Noir

Chat Noir is Ladybug's partner,he appeared at the same time as her and they have been partners ever since there first fight together against Stoneheart. He is a cat-themed hero, and his magic jewel is a ring. He has all the same powers as Ladybug except he doesn't have a lucky charm, but cataclysm which gives him the power to destroy everything he comes into contact with. Rena Rouge

Rena Rouge made her first debut as a fox themed superhero while helping Ladybug and Chat Noir capture the Akuma belonging to the Sapotis. She has advanced fighting skills, enchanted speed, strength, and agility. She uses her magical object (a flute) as a weapon, by calling on her mirage power. Her magic jewel is a necklace shaped like a fox tail, which was given to our mysterious hero by the one and only Ladybug herself. Although her identity remains unknown she has proven her self trustworthy not only by coming to Ladybug's aid when asked but by returning the miraculous after her mission is over.
