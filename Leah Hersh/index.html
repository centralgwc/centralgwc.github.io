Sloths are adorable and underrated creatures. Sloths are great. I like sloths. Here are some facts you might not know about sloths:

  • They sleep for 15-28 hours a day.
  • When they go into the water, algae cling to their fur, camoflaging them.
  • They are excellent swimmers.
  • They can have two or three toes depending on the species.
